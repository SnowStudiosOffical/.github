## About Us

Welcome to **SnowStudios** – where we push the boundaries of virtual reality gaming. Founded in 2024, we're a passionate team dedicated to creating immersive VR experiences that blur the line between virtual and reality.

We aren't a typical company—as a flat organization without hierarchy and job titles, we believe in creative freedom, collaboration, and pushing technological boundaries. Every game we create is designed from the ground up for VR, ensuring the most authentic and immersive experiences possible.

## Join Our Team

We're always looking for talented individuals who share our passion for VR. At SnowStudios, opportunities run broad and deep across the following areas:

### **Software Engineer**
Build cutting-edge VR systems using Unity, Unreal Engine, and custom technologies. Work on gameplay mechanics, optimization, and innovative features that define next-gen VR experiences.

**Skills we're looking for:**
- Unity/Unreal Engine experience
- VR development knowledge
- C#/C++ proficiency
- Strong problem-solving abilities

### **3D Art & Animation**
Create stunning 3D environments, characters, and animations that bring our VR worlds to life. Master tools like Blender, Maya, and Substance to craft visually breathtaking experiences.

**Skills we're looking for:**
- 3D modeling and texturing
- Character/environment art
- Animation expertise
- Understanding of VR optimization

### **Game Designer**
Design engaging gameplay systems, levels, and mechanics specifically for VR. Balance creativity with player comfort to create experiences that are both innovative and accessible.

**Skills we're looking for:**
- VR game design experience
- Level design proficiency
- Understanding of player psychology
- Prototyping abilities
